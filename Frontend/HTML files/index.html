About Codeup

CSE Coding Club proudly presents CodeUp, an exhilarating full-day event tailored exclusively for second-year students. Set to ignite the passion for coding and innovation, CodeUp promises a day filled with excitement, challenges, and opportunities to showcase coding prowess. CodeUp comprises four engaging events designed to push the boundaries of participants' coding skills and foster creativity in problem-solving. CodeUp isn't just about competition; it's also an excellent opportunity for networking, learning, and collaboration. Throughout the day, participants will interact with peers, mentors, and industry professionals, gaining valuable insights and forging lasting connections. So, gear up, second-year students, for a day packed with coding challenges, learning experiences, and the chance to showcase your coding prowess at CodeUp, brought to you by the CSE Coding Club.
